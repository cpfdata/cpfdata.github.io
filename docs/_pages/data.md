## Basic information about CPF data

CPF version 1.0. covers the period until 2018, combines seven countries, and includes in total around 2.7 million observations from almost 360 thousand respondents. The oldest survey is PSID which started in 1968 and collected 40 waves until now. The second oldest is SOPE which started in 1984 and collected 35 waves.
